What is activate.uhc.com?
------------------------
activate.uhc.com is the official online portal where UnitedHealthcare members can activate their UHC account and access plan details.

Things You Need Before Activation
---------------------------------
- UHC Member ID card  
- Registered email address  
- Date of birth  
- Internet-enabled device  

How to Activate UHC Using Member ID
----------------------------------
1. Open your web browser.
2. Go to **activate.uhc.com**.
3. Click on **Activate Account**.
4. Enter your UHC Member ID.
5. Verify your date of birth.
6. Create a username and password.
7. Submit the form.
8. Your UHC account is activated.

What You Can Do After Activation
--------------------------------
- View your digital UHC ID card  
- Check claims and coverage  
- Find doctors and hospitals  
- Manage your health plan online  

Common Activation Issues
------------------------
- **Member ID not working:** Recheck the ID from your card.  
- **Verification failed:** Confirm your date of birth.  
- **Site not loading:** Try another browser or clear cache.  

FAQs
----
**Can I activate UHC on mobile?**  
Yes, activate.uhc.com works on mobile devices.

**Is Member ID required?**  
Yes, Member ID is mandatory for activation.

**How long does activation take?**  
Only a few minutes.
